AKAN NAME

Get to know the meaning of the term Akan name

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. This site was specifically made for you...We are hear to give clarity and satisfy your curiosity.
Here are Ghanian day names:

Male

Sunday:Kwasi

Monday:Kwadwo

Tuesday:Kwabena

Wednesday:Kwaku

Thursday:Yaw

Friday:Kofi

Saturday:Kwame

Female

Sunday:Akosua

Monday:Adwoa

Tuesday:Abenaa

Wednesday:Akua

Thursday:Yaa

Friday:Afua

Saturday:Ama
